Sizzling Tofu Recipe

Description

This recipe requires very few and simple ingredients. Firm tofu, bell peppers, garlic, onions, mayonnaise, and oyster sauce is actually all you need. But if you like, you can add some chilis to give it an added kick.

I am used to frying tofu as a whole block and just cutting it once it is fried, this is how it is done usually, well in our house at least. It is easier and faster that way, especially if you are gonna fry a lot of tofu.

Ingredients

  • Firm Tofu
  • Bell Peppers
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Mayonnaise
  • Oyster Sauce

Steps

  1. Fry the block of tofu. You can also slice it before frying altogether.
  2. In a sizzling plate or cast iron pan, saute garlic, onions, and bell peppers in butter for a minute - or until onions become translucent.
  3. Add in the mayonnaise, oyster sauce, water, green peppers, and chili (optional).
  4. Let it cook and simmer while stirring for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Toss in the fried tofu and mix until well-blended. Serve while hot on the sizzling plate or cast iron pan.
